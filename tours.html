Single Night

Join one of our guides in the beautiful nature that is Lapland in the north of Sweden. You will arrive at the hotel in the afternoon and go through some of te details for the evening, familiarizing yourself with the camera equipment. After a 3 course meal it's time to head out either by car, scoorter or foot depending on the conditions and experience some of best views the north has to offer.

This package is for those who have little time or are perhaps passing through and want to take advantage of the the local to watch the Aurora. Up to 4 people can join in order to preserve the quality of the tour.

The price is 2000Kr and this include lodgings, all meals and travel insurance.

Adventure tour

This package is for those who are more adventerous and wish to experience the true wilderness of the north. Armed with a camera and a backapack we will spend 4-7 days depending on the wishes of the people involed camping and trecking to some wonderful sights.

No previous experience is required and all equipement needed will be provided, if you wish to bring your own equipment please let us know in advance so we can verify if they are suited for the trip.

The price ranges from 6000-8000kr depending on the duration of the tour.

Luxury Tour

This trip spans 3 nights and 3 days where you will lodging at a 4 star hotel all inclusive, and take several trips to different locations to experience the Aurora Borealis in all its glory. During the day a variety of excursions will be planned to explore the local wildlife and nature.

The trip is fully customizable depending on the wishes of the people booking, only a single person or party will be able to book at one time to make it as exclusive as possible. This package can be booked for anything ranging from a business trip with the company or even a honeymoon.

Price will depend on size of party and activities chosen.
