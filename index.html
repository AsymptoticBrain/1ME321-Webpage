Welcome to our webpage "Aurora hunting" here we showcase some of our images from the Aurora Borealis and information as to what causes these amazing sights. For those interested we offer special tours in the north of Sweden where you will be accompanied by experienced guides and get hands on experience in photographing the Northren Lights.

The picture above shows the aurora as seen from the International Space Station, it truely shows how large of a phenomenon it is. While perhaps not quite as grandiose, the view from the ground is each and everytime a unique experience.
